About

In the lively fabric of South Africa's music scene, Njumanjie Entertainment emerged in 2021, ignited by the passion and vision of the dynamic artist Ramphelane Mohlomphegi Mphela, affectionately known as "Hlomphi." Our ethos is simple yet profound: we are not just a record label; we are curators of culture, seekers of raw talent, and architects of dreams. From the infectious beats of Dollar S.A to the lyrical prowess of Siga R.S.A, the trap-infused melodies of Trapiano, the electrifying energy of Dizytouch, and the visionary artistry of Hlomphi himself, our roster embodies diversity, innovation, and excellence.

Who are we?

we are more than just a label; we are a movement dedicated to unearthing and championing the next wave of musical luminaries from the streets of South Africa. Our journey begins on the gritty pavements where undiscovered brilliance dwells, and it culminates in the global stage where our artists shine brightest. Driven by a team of passionate and relentless individuals, we work tirelessly, day and night, to empower our artists to reach unprecedented heights. Our mission is clear: to harness the untapped potential of raw talent and sculpt it into something extraordinary, something that resonates with hearts and minds across the globe.

Tag along!

Our journey is far from over. As we continue to write the next chapter of South Africa's musical legacy, we invite you to join us, to tune in and turn up the volume on the voices that define our generation. This is more than just music; this is a movement. This is Njumanjie Entertainment. Welcome to the future of sound.
